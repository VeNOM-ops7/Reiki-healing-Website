Pendulum Dowsing

Pendulum dowsing is a spiritual tool used to receive intuitive answers from the subconscious or higher self. The pendulum reacts to subtle energies and helps bring clarity to yes/no questions.

Observe the pendulum as it swings. Calm your mind, ask a question silently, and watch the motion—it may guide your answer.

This ancient divination technique is often used for spiritual guidance, energy healing, and decision-making.
