Reiki Healing

Reiki is a gentle, hands-on healing technique that originates from Japan. It is based on the idea that energy flows through us and that this energy can be channeled to promote physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being.

During a Reiki session, the practitioner channels energy through their hands to the recipient, helping to clear energy blockages, reduce stress, and encourage natural healing processes.

This holistic approach complements other medical treatments and can be beneficial for anyone seeking balance and harmony in their life.
